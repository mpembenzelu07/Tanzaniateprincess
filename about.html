About Us

Our Story

Tanzanite Princess FC was born in Temeke, Keko, Dar es Salaam, with a vision to nurture young women through football. We are a community built on shared commitment, purpose, and the pursuit of excellence. Our official page reflects our journey – from local pitches to the TFF U17 Women's League championship.
