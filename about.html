About Us · Kuhusu Sisi

Our Story

Tanzanite Princess FC was founded with a vision to empower young women through football. Based in Temeke, Keko, Dar es Salaam, we provide professional training and a path to excellence. Our academy is a place of shared commitment, purpose, and growth.

We believe in fair play, positivity, and community. Our motto: “Play fair, Be Positive” – “Cheza haki, Kuwa na chanya”.

Our Values

Striving for the highest level on and off the pitch.

For teammates, opponents, and the game.
